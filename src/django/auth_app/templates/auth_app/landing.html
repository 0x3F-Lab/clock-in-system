Chronex keeps hospitality management simple.

Chronex is a lightweight staff management platform designed with simplicity at its core. Built for small hospitality businesses, it helps you roster shifts, track hours, and manage the essentials without unnecessary complexity. The interface is quick to learn and easy to use, with only the tools that matter. While it is ideal for small teams, Chronex is flexible enough to support larger venues that want a clean, straightforward system without confusion.
